Tzatziki (Greek Yogurt & Cucumber Dip)

Description

Tzatziki is a cool and creamy Greek dip made with yogurt, cucumber, garlic, and olive oil. It's delicious as a dip, a sauce for grilled meats, or spread on warm pita bread.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup Greek yogurt
  • 1/2 cucumber, grated and drained
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tbsp vinegar or lemon juice
  • 1 tbsp chopped fresh dill (optional)
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Grate the cucumber and squeeze out as much water as you can using a clean towel.
  2. In a bowl, combine the yogurt, cucumber, garlic, olive oil, vinegar or lemon juice, and dill.
  3. Mix well, then season with salt and pepper to taste.
  4. Chill in the fridge for at least 30 minutes before serving to let the flavors blend.
