Greek Salad

Simple goes perfect with meat or accompanying a pita gyros

All you need is
  • 1 cucumber
  • 2 cloves of garlic minced
  • 1/4 of a cup extra virgin olive oil
  • 500g strained Greek yogurt
  • Lots of olive oil
  • A big piece of feta cheese
  • Salt
Steps for the preparation
  1. Cucumber should be grated with no water left
  2. Mixed with the yoghurt
  3. The 2 cloves of garlic should be minced
  4. Put them into a bowl and mix well
  5. Add a generous amount of olive oil
  6. Secret: The more olive oil the tastier so do not be afraid to add more
  7. Add some salt and gently mix them
