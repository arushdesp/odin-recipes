Tirokafteri (Spicy Greek Feta Dip)

Description

Tirokafteri is a creamy and spicy Greek feta dip, blended with roasted peppers and a touch of chili. It's perfect as a meze, a spread for bread, or a dip for veggies.

Ingredients

  • 200g feta cheese
  • 1-2 roasted red peppers
  • 2 tbsp Greek yogurt
  • 1-2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 small chili pepper (or pinch of chili flakes)
  • 1 tbsp lemon juice
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Crumble the feta cheese into a bowl.
  2. Add the chopped roasted peppers, yogurt, olive oil, chili, and lemon juice.
  3. Blend or mash everything together until smooth and creamy.
  4. Season with salt and pepper to your liking.
  5. Chill before serving for the best flavor.
