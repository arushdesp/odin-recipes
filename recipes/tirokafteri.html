    Greek Salad

    Simple goes perfect with meat or accompanying a pita gyros

    All you need is
    • 50 g olive oil
    • 1/2 chili pepper
    • A spoon or two of paprika
    • 400 g feta cheese
    • 200 g strained yogurt
    • Salt
    Steps for the preparation
    1. Mix in a blender oil,pepper,chilli flakes,paprika,bit salt
    2. Mixed with the yoghurt and the feta chopped
    3. Put everything in bowl and mix gently
    4. Finish it with some more olive oil
