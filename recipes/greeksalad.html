Greek Salad

Or else Horiatiki Salata as we say in my village, an easy to make salad, goes perfect with meat and is good to eat for dinner

All you need is
  • 3 tomatoes
  • 1 medium red onion
  • 1 cucumber
  • 1 green pepper (optional)
  • Olives according to preference
  • Lots of olive oil
  • A big piece of feta cheese
  • Origano and bit salt
Steps for the preparation
  1. Cut tomatoes into wedges
  2. Slice onion into rings
  3. Slice cucumber into half-moons
  4. Put them into a big bowl
  5. Put last in the bowl the piece of feta
  6. Secret: The more olive oil the tastier so put a lot
  7. Gently mix them and finish with some origano and salt
