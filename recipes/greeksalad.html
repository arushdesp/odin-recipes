Greek Salad (Horiatiki Salata)

Description

A classic, easy-to-make Greek salad that's fresh, vibrant, and full of flavor. It's perfect as a side for grilled meats or as a light meal on its own.

Ingredients

  • 3 tomatoes
  • 1 medium red onion
  • 1 cucumber
  • 1 green pepper (optional)
  • Olives (to taste)
  • Lots of olive oil
  • A big piece of feta cheese
  • Oregano and a bit of salt

Instructions

  1. Cut the tomatoes into wedges.
  2. Slice the onion into thin rings.
  3. Slice the cucumber into half-moons.
  4. Add all the vegetables to a large bowl.
  5. Place the feta cheese on top.
  6. Drizzle generously with olive oilâ€”don't be shy, it makes all the difference!
  7. Sprinkle with oregano and a pinch of salt.
  8. Gently toss everything together and serve right away.
